About Me

This is what I do.

Hello! I’m Abraham Obubo. I’m a Software Engineer with a background in Mechatronics Engineering. My journey into programming began during my undergraduate studies, where I explored circuit components, microcontrollers, and embedded systems. This hands-on experience gave me a deep understanding of how computers work at a fundamental level and sparked my interest in robotics and software development.

Through this blog, I share my thoughts on engineering, computer science, and cool tech stuff that excites me. Whether you’re into building things, coding, or just curious about how technology shapes our world, I hope you find something interesting here. Feel free to reach out—I’d love to connect and hear your thoughts!
